  • Schläfli symbol {p,q} ↔ p-gons, q per vertex
  • Total angle: 2π
  • Angle per polygon: π(p-2)/p
  • (π(p-2)/p)⋅q ≤ 2π
  • (p-2)⋅(q-2) ≤ 4

Algorithmic Generation

  • Each tile has exactly 7 neighbours
  • A tile and its neighbour share exactly 2 neighbours

propagate(Tile t):
	n = len(t.neighbours)
	new_neighbours = []
	for i in range(7-n):
		u = Tile()
		link(t,u)
		new_neighbours.append(u)
	m = len(new_neighbours)
	for i in range(m):
		link(new_neighbours[i],new_neighbours[(i+1)%m])
	link(new_neighbours[0],neighbours[0])
	link(new_neighbours[n-1],neighbours[m-1])
